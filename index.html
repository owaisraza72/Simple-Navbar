Social Networks

The phrase social network refers to a social structure made up of individuals or organizations. It develops friendly ties amoung people. Through social networking, a man , agroup , a party , an organization ,or an instition can introduce themselves and get propagated. The best feature of social networking is that it expose its members to people all over the world. It confirms the fact that people are the same through the globe.
